ROUTES

Route 66, USA

An iconic American road trip stretching from Chicago to Santa Monica, spanning over 3,900 km. It showcases classic Americana, small towns, desert vistas, and a rich cultural history.

The Great Ocean Road, Australia

This coastal road covers about 250 km from Torquay to Allansford, offering spectacular ocean views, the famous Twelve Apostles, rainforests, and wildlife.

Ruta 40, Patagonia, Argentina

One of the longest road trips in the world, Ruta 40 runs nearly 5,000 km along the Andes, taking in glaciers, lakes, and the wilds of Argentine Patagonia.

Garden Route, South Africa

This legendary route travels 200 km along the southern coast between Mossel Bay and Storms River, packed with wildlife encounters, adventure activities, and stunning views.
